Why Donate Blood?

  • Save Lives

    A single donation can save up to 3 lives.

  • Health Check

    Free mini health check-up during donation process.

  • Reduce Health Risks

    Regular donation reduces risk of heart and liver ailments.

  • Blood Regeneration

    Stimulates production of new blood cells.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age

    Must be at least 18 years old.

  • Weight

    Must weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).

  • Time Between Donations

    At least 180 days since your last donation.

  • Health

    Must be in good general health and feeling well.
